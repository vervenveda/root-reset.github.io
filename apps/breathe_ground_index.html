Breath & Ground

Any Time Reset · 4–4–6 Rhythm

A gentle, all-day breathing practice to calm your nervous system and bring you back into your body. Follow the circle as it expands to inhale, softens to hold, and settles as you exhale. You can use this for one minute or several — whenever you need to arrive, release, and reset.

Ready
4
When you’re ready, press Begin and follow the rhythm.

Suggested pattern: Inhale for 4 · Hold for 4 · Exhale for 6. If you feel light-headed, soften the inhale or pause the practice. You can close your eyes once you know the rhythm and just listen to your breath.
