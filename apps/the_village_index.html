Welcome to The Village

Think of this as a tiny constellation of places inside you.

  • Click a tile (Rest, Boundaries, Creativity, Help) to visit it.
  • Each visit warms that node, sends a soft pulse, and logs it locally.
  • Use the Dev button (bottom-right) to gently tune the visuals and particles.
  • Enable Sound if you’d like quiet chimes and optional voice.

Nothing expires. Nothing judges. Everything waits until you’re ready.
